Names lead your mind along the path of least resistance.

Is OpenAI open?

It feels that the answer must be "Yes". It's right there in the name "OpenAI". Which is the point: the name leads your mind toward the pre-determined conclusion someone else wanted you to reach. Not, necessarily, toward the truth.

If asked whether "XQ-17 Holdings" was open, you would immediately realize you don't know. But "OpenAI" makes you feel as though you do even though you probably know as much of the inner workings of one as of the other. The label creates an illusion of knowledge.

Notice how the question itself sounds like a contradiction — as if it were wrong to ask it. Is OpenAI not open? The discomfort is the engineering working as intended.
